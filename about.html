Our Story

Too Good To Go is one of Europe's most promising start-ups. We’ve made a massive impact in reducing food waste and our story has been well covered by international, national and local medias. We’re currently active in 8 European countries with more on the way!

In less than 2 years we've partnered with over 5,000 stores to fight food waste, 3 million people have downloaded the app, and over 2.5 million meals have been rescued.
